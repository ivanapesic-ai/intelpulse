## Technology Maturity Visualization Platform for ML-SDV Sphere

**Submitted to:** BluSpecs  
**Date:** December 2025  
**Project Duration:** 3 months  
**Total Investment:** €18,500

## Executive Summary

We propose to deliver a production-ready Technology Maturity Heatmap platform that enables BluSpecs to offer a paid visualization service to public authorities and industry experts. The solution combines dual visualization approaches (Radar Chart + Heatmap), AI-powered data extraction, and a sustainable admin-managed access model—all hosted on EU infrastructure with ISO 27000 alignment.

**Key Benefits:**
- Low-risk market validation with minimal upfront investment
- 3 months to first paying clients
- EU compliance built-in (data residency, security)
- Scalable from 10 to 10,000 users

## Proposed Solution

### Dual Visualization System

| Visualization | Purpose | Key Features |
|---------------|---------|--------------|
| **Radar Chart** | Quick technology comparison | Multi-axis scoring, overlay comparison, exportable |
| **Heatmap Matrix** | Detailed maturity landscape | TRL levels × Technology domains, drill-down capability |

### Access Tiers

| Tier | Features |
|------|----------|
| **Public Demo** | Sample data, basic filters, lead capture, no auth required |
| **Premium** | Full ML-SDV coverage, advanced filters, data export, BluSpecs-managed account |

### Admin Capabilities
Add/remove users, grant/revoke access, view analytics, trigger data refresh—no payment integration needed.

### AI-Powered Processing
Automatic extraction from PDF/PPT, structured parsing, technology categorization, TRL assessment support.

## Scope & Assumptions

### BluSpecs Responsibilities
1. Provide Dealroom API access and credentials
2. Specify CEI data format and delivery method
3. Provide domain expertise for TRL assessments
4. Handle client contracts and access grants
5. Review deliverables within 5 business days

### Out of Scope
❌ Payment integration (handled externally)  
❌ Automated data polling (manual refresh)  
❌ Native mobile apps  
❌ Multi-sphere expansion (future phase)  
❌ Real-time collaboration  
❌ Custom analytics dashboards

### Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| CEI data format unclear | Early clarification; flexible import |
| Dealroom API limitations | Fallback to manual entry |
| Scope creep | Clear phase gates; change process |
